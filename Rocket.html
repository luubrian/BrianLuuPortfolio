Process and Features

Nozzle Design


Using RPA, inputted an arbitrary thrust force, and then modeled the rocket nozzle shape.

Nozzle RPA Analysis


RPA outputted dimensions that would shape the nozzle, as well as, describing different values.

Injector Design


My greatest challenge was a lack of online sources that taught me how to develop a Coaxial Swirler. I based my design mainly on Copenhagen Suborbitals' swirler design, while sourcing dimensions from other collegiate rocket projects and NASA.

Injector Plate


Designed an additional housing that would allow the numerous injectors to be attached to the nozzle design above.

Coaxial Swirler Injector Testing


Used ANSYS Fluent to develop velocity streamlines and flow lines that described fluid motion through the injector.
